About Jervis

Jervis is a Python model developed to make your life easier by recognizing voice commands to open various websites and perform specific tasks. Whether you need to get the latest news updates or enjoy a song, Jervis is here to help!

Features

- Opens different websites based on audio commands
- Tells the latest news
- Sings a song upon request

Special thanks to ChatGPT and YouTube platforms for their invaluable assistance and resources in developing Jervis.
